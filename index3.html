How to Prepare Dosa Batter

In this recipe, you will learn how to make dosa batter.


Ingredients


  • 3 parts idly/dosa rice (1 cup) (the bigger round one). Can use any short grain rice, but not sure if the results will be same
  • 1 part whole urad dal (1/3 cup)
  • 2 tbsp chana dal
  • 1 tsp methi
  • Soak for 5 hours, then fine grind in mixer grinder (the big jar)
  • Keep at warm area over night.
