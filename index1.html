How To Prepare Chapatis


In this recipe, you will learn how to make a chapati.


  1. Mix one cup wheat flour, 1/4th teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon oil well.
  2. Add some water and knead the dough.
  3. Take a small portion of the dough, and roll it into flat chapatis.
  4. Fry your chapati on the pan.
